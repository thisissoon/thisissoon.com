Peabody Sales

Responsive website for the new sales arm of Peabody property

A new paradigm in property sales.

Peabody is a remarkable organisation, having provided sustainable, affordable, lifetime homes in London for over 150 years. That’s why when we started working with the newly formed Peabody sales team, we knew they would be equally remarkable - investing 100% of profits from the sale residential properties back in to affordable housing & community support services. With such a unique positioning, passionate beliefs and individual approach we were focused from the outset in ensuring this shone through brightly in how the website engages and supports customers.

To deliver on this it was essential to understand the tension between Peabody’s social purpose as a registered charity and their new position as a credible - and above all, premium - property sales business. We needed to deliver not just a great search & selection experience for customers, but to follow their journeys from first contact, viewings and throughout the sales process. We also worked alongside Peabody’s partner agency Moore-Wilson, who were engaged to build the site with SOON providing additional creative direction and QA throughout the project.

The customer is always right

From the outset we acknowledged the role of mobile & tablet. We created a fully responsive design that adapts to any device the customer is likely to use, in any stage of their purchase journey.

Search & selection

Giving customers the ability to elegantly and quickly search, sort and filter properties was a priority, respecting user’s needs and ensuring no time is wasted.

Visualising lifestyle

Property sales is as much about lifestyle as it is about bricks and mortar. Bringing the local area and communities around the developments to life was essential – with more feature content to follow in the future.

Consistently individual

Every development is different – both in it’s location, ambience, architectural design and the visual identity created to market it. Our design approach allowed the development pages to feel tonally very different and unique, showcasing their individuality.

Individually consistent

Despite this variation, the design approach maintains a strict consistency – so even users viewing many developments that feel tonally different, will have a user experience that is consistent, structured and reassuring.

1/5

Palette & Icons

Evolving new meaning from an established brand A new complimentary colour-set allowed us to add richness to the palette without abandoning the core Peabody brand. This was complimented by a new set of iconography that feels premium but approachable.

Consistent design, infinitely adaptable

CMS driven templates allow the unique character of each development to shine through.

An online sales experience that just works

The needs of customers change throughout the sales process and understanding their requirements, mindset and likely choice of device at each stage of the journey was essential to delivering a great end-to-end sales experience.

This meant an adaptable interface that can un-pack over time, allowing the customer dashboard to develop in complexity as prospects make initial contact with sales personnel, arrange viewings, make an offer and manage legal proceedings through to completion.

It also meant juggling some complex and detailed functional requirements with elegant, premium, friendly graphic design - so the website provides customers with what they need, when they need it, in the most convenient way possible.
