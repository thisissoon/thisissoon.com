Peabody Sales

Branding and website for the new sales arm of Peabody property.

Moving a charitable trust into luxury sales.

The Peabody Trust is a remarkable organisation, having provided sustainable, affordable, lifetime homes in London for over 150 years. That’s why when we started working with the newly formed Peabody Sales group, we knew they would be equally remarkable - investing profits from their commercial sales back in to affordable housing and community support services. With such a unique positioning, passionate beliefs and individual approach we were focussed from the outset in ensuring this shone through brightly in the new brand & in the way the website supports and engages customers.

To deliver on this, it was essential to understand the tension between Peabody’s traditional role as a charitable trust and their new positioning as a credible - and above all, premium - luxury property sales business. Importantly, we needed to deliver not just a great research & selection experience for customers, but to follow their journeys through to first contact, viewings and all throughout the sales process. We also worked alongside an established Peabody agency Moore-Wilson, who were engaged to build the site, assisting with creative direction and ongoing QA throughout the project.

An online sales experience that just works

The needs of customers change throughout the sales process and understanding their requirements, mindset and likely choice of device at each stage of the journey was essential to delivering a great end-to-end sales experience.

This meant an adaptable interface that can un-pack over time, allowing the customer dashboard to develop in complexity as prospects make initial contact with sales personnel, arrange viewings, make an offer and manage legal proceedings through to completion.

It also meant juggling some complex and detailed functional requirements with elegant, premium, friendly graphic design - so the website provides customers with what they need, when they need it, in the most convenient way possible.
