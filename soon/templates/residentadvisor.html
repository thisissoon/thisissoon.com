Resident Advisor

A new visual identity & platform re-design for club culture’s favourite online magazine.

Evolving a much loved brand.

Resident Advisor (RA) is an online music magazine that's dedicated to showcasing electronic music, artists and events across the globe. It also has one of the most passionate and dedicated communities we’ve ever encountered - for whom RA is an hourly destination, with all the heated debates and digital culture that entails.

We weren’t just re-branding a company or re-designing a website. We were diving in to people’s lives, looking to re-invent something that was part of their daily routines and online identity.

That meant approaching the project with an exceptional level of consideration and respect for the community - but still being unrepentant about what we believed needed to change.

This could only have been achieved by working hand-in-glove with the inspirational and talented management of RA, who develop the entire site in-house.
