Resident Advisor

A new visual identity & platform re-design for club culture’s favourite online magazine.

The changing face of music.

Resident Advisor (RA) is an online music magazine dedicated to showcasing electronic music, artists and events across the globe. Throughout the project, we worked collaboratively and strategically with the inspirational management of RA who develop the entire site in-house.

How we access, consume and interact with music has changed radically since RA’s launch in 2001 and the new site acknowledges this through its responsive design, an emphasis on de-cluttering & simplifying the interface, by the evolution of the brand and by focussing on bringing RA’s exceptional, exclusive content to the fore.

Responsive web platform

The impact of mobile Web necessitated a completely new approach to the platform - taking in to account the new way people use their devices to consume and share opinions on music.

A place for everything

Strong colour coding not only freshens the brand identity, but allowed us to reduce the complexity of the site by clearly delineating editorial, events, music and user content.

Content first

The minimal, structured design allowed us to be as economical as possible with graphic elements. Nothing is decorative, everything lays focus on RA’s outstanding original writing & films.

Improved events

Events were radically overhauled. The new commenting system relates discussion threads to the content that triggered the debate, rather than isolating it in a traditional forum.

Advanced profiles

Cleaner, stronger, more confident design in the user profiles sections means a vastly simplified interface and more powerful features for RA’s dedicated community of users.
